We Drive Sustainable Solutions for a Greener Future

Green Treat is a leading provider of innovative and eco-friendly solutions for sewage, water, landscaping, and civil and construction works

Company History

Established in 2011, Green Treat has been dedicated to delivering sustainable solutions to homes, offices, estates, and entire districts in Nigeria and Africa.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to drive innovation and promote environmental sustainability through our services. We envision a greener future where we contribute to healthier communities.

Our Values

Green Treat is committed to:

Innovation

We are continuously seeking new technologies and approaches.

Quality

We are always delivering high-quality services that exceed customer expectations.

Sustainability

We are always prioritizing eco-friendly practices and minimizing environmental impact.

Customer Satisfaction

We are building long-lasting relationships through exceptional service.
